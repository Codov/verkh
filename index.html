    Slip and Fall

    Our slip and fall accident lawyers at Verkhovets Law know that you can trip and fall anywhere at any time.

    At a restaurant, you can slip and fall over the puckered carpeting. At a department store, a pool of water on the washroom tile sends you flying backward. Or if an icy plaza in front of your barber shop isn’t salted, then you’re face-down.

    To be successful in litigation, your slip and fall injury must be the result of someone’s negligence. That’s the key.

    Dog Bites, Animal Attacks

    The Toronto dog bite lawyers at Verkhovets Law know how terrifying it is to experience an encounter with a vicious dog. One minute you’re strolling with your labradoodle on his leash and out of nowhere, you’re fending off a horrific dog bite attack – leaving both you and your dog with serious injuries.

    Apologies or denials of blame don’t matter.

    The law in Ontario is clear: the owner automatically must cover your damages and/or your dog’s veterinary bills.

    Brain Injuries

    The Toronto brain injury lawyers at Verkhovets Law know that a traumatic brain injury is always very serious.

    Once deprived of oxygen, a victim’s brain can have problems with eyesight, movement (or paralysis), inability to focus, memory problems, migraine headaches, and seizures as a result of a traumatic brain injury.

    Traumatic brain injuries are common in the most serious car accidents, slip and fall accidents, as well as diving and swimming pool accidents.

    Wrongful Death

    The Toronto wrongful death lawyers at Verkhovets Law know how devastating it is to lose a loved one in an untimely way due to someone else’s negligence.

    Emotions are swirling: grief, anger, despair. And then there are financial worries: how will you pay your bills and put the children through school?

    A wrongful death claim is a legal remedy for immediate family, dependents, spouses, life partners, and others suffering substantial emotional damages from the loss of a loved one.

    The Verkhovets

    Difference

    The personal injury lawyers at Verkhovets Law put our clients first. That means:

    1. We offer an initial 1-hour consultation at no charge. We will meet you at your home, hospital or other place of convenience if you are unable to attend our office for the consultation.
    2. Once we accept your case, we will continue to make your life easier by co-ordinating with your treatment providers and collaborating with everyone involved in your care. We do this for all our clients.
    3. We take the time to get to know our clients, build trust, and a level of comfort in our working relationship.
    4. We communicate regularly with our clients and take steps to minimize the impact of the injury on various aspects of their life, and make the process of medical, emotional and financial recovery as smooth and easy as possible.
    5. Together, we will build a solid foundation to your successful recovery.
    6. We are paid on contingency. This means that you will not have to incur legal fees or out-of-pocket costs. We will bear all the expenses associated with your case until the settlement, award, or judgment in your case. We get paid when you get paid.
