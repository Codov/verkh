Verkhovets Law, personal injury lawyers, works with accident victims who have been injured as a result of some else’s negligence, including:

   Bicycle accidents

   Car Accidents

   Slip and Falls

   Spinal Cord Injuries

   Dog Bites and Animal Attacks

   Long-Term Disability Claims

   Mortgage Insurance Claims

The personal injury lawyers at Verkhovets Law work:

1) to obtain the medical evidence/reports you need to file a successful personal injury claim if your case has merit,

2) to get the rehabilitation, physiotherapy, and other supports for your successful recovery to the extent medically possible

3) and to get the compensation you deserve to help you adjust to your new reality.
